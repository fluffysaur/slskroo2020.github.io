Ponya

Ponya Privilege!

Previous Next

Arguably the cutest animal out of all five houses, the Red Panda lives amongst the treetops. Similarly, The House of Ponya occupies the top three floors of Tembusu and is home to the most welcoming bunch of people. Many might call this close knit group a cult, but the only real cult following in this house is for a friendly game of Mahjong. You’ll see this lively bunch take the wearisome journey down the tower for the many breakfast, lunch and dinner ‘jios’. Travelling as a pack, the lift journey no longer is boring but filled with laughter, banter and even the odd selfie. Other than eating together, you’ll also often see them playing badminton to shake off the stresses of the day.

And when night falls the camaraderie only continues as the house is filled with music, warmth and the chittering of Ponyans.
