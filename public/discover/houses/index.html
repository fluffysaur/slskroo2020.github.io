Houses

Established in 2011, the five houses are each named after an endangered Asian animal species. But why?

Tembusu aims to create a culture of commitment to conservation, biodiversity, and the ethical treatment of sentient beings. As such, we want to instil a sense of concern and appreciation for animals and the environment.

Due to the COVID-19 situation, the houses will be hibernating for this semester.
